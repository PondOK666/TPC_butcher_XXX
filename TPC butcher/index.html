Types of Beef Cuts

Chunk

The chuck primal consists of the shoulder and neck region of the cow. Due to its high amount of connective tissue and fat content.

Rib

The rib primal consists of the top part of the center section of rib, specifically the sixth through the twelfth ribs of the cow.

Loin

The loin primal is located behind the ribs and it is not a heavily used muscle, which makes for some of the most tender meat on the cow.

Round

The round primal consists of beef cuts that come from the rump, hips, hind legs, and knees of the cow.
